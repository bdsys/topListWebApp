What is Top List?

The goal is to create a social website for people to "like" or "dislike" items in specific catagories that will either raise to the top or drop to the bottom of the "top lists". Users will create accounts and have friends and groups. Online Merchants can pay a small fee to have a "merchant account" to sell and display various goods through a specified top list.


We were inspired to start building Top List after looking for fruitlessly looking for vinyl records online. Andrew was learning HTML, CSS and JavaScript on Codecademy and started building away. The small learning project soon alligned with Andrew's systems engineering project, Black Dog Systems.

Why so many cats?

Well, simply put, Top List is still in Alpha testing. This means that the application is still under active development and not close to done. Anywhere you see the word "cat", a picture of a "cat" or anything to do with a cat, you can assume that we're using it as a "place holder". This means that we put the cat there to remind us that something legitimant needs to go there and a cat makes us smile and is pleasent to look at (Much more so than blank space or something random).


If you see a cat somewhere, let us know! We will let you know what is intended to go there. Who knows, if you let us know about a cat place holder we have forgotten, you may be rewarded!
